# 🍽️ Zomato Restaurant Analysis

I analyzed Zomato's restaurant dataset containing 9,500+ 
restaurants across multiple cities in India to uncover 
real business insights.

## What I Explored
- Which cities have the most restaurants
- What cuisines are most popular across India
- How customers rate restaurants
- What percentage of restaurants offer online delivery

## Key Findings
- New Delhi dominates with 5,000+ restaurants
- Only 25% restaurants offer online delivery — 
  a huge untapped market opportunity
- North Indian cuisine is the most popular
- Majority of restaurants are rated "Good" or "Very Good"

## Tech Stack
Python | Pandas | Matplotlib | Seaborn
